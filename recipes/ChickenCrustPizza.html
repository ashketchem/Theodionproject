Chicken Crust Pizza

Ingredients


  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped green peppers

The crust is made from canned chicken. Canned chicken! It’s really brilliant if you think about It. Now, you can do one of two things: you can blend/pulse up the crust ingredients in a food processor. This method obviously grinds and blends everything up until smooth. If you are not a fan of texture, you will want to do this. If you’re like me, and want to taste the chicken and have texture, simply mash and mix everything together by hand; no food processor needed.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground chicken, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Form the chicken mixture into a 10-inch circle on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Remove from the oven and spread with pizza sauce.
  6. Top with mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, onions, and green peppers.
  7. Bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
